input

Dia-detect

dia-gnosis

After considering all inputs, we display a prediction as to whether or not the patient has Diabetes 2, along with a % probability for your reference.

Disclaimer

This program is not intended to give professional medical advice. The AI may occasionally have false judgements. Please fill out all fields possible.

Training data

We trained the neural network off of the Pima Indians Diabetes Database by The National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases.

There are risks with undetected diabetes.

Out of the 37.3 million people across the United States, ​8.5 million people (23.0% of adults) go undiagnosed for diabetes. These people are at risk of complications such as heart attack, stroke, kidney problems, and cancer.

Source: CDC National Diabetes Statistics Report

Enter Patient Information

Pregnancies -- Number of times pregnant.


Glucose -- Plasma glucose concentration after 2 hours in an oral glucose tolerance test.


Blood Pressure -- Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg).


Skin Thickness -- Triceps skin fold thickness (mm).


Insulin -- 2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/ml).


BMI -- Body mass index (weight in kg/(height in m)^2).


Diabetes Pedigree Function -- Function that scores the likelihood of diabetes based on family history.


Age -- Age in years.
